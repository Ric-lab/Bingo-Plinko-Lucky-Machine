MVP Release: Bingo Plinko Lucky Machine

Development Phase Complete. This commit solidifies the MVP with the following features and implementations:

1. Core Gameplay
- Hybrid Bingo-Plinko mechanics: Balls drop through a physics-based peg board (Matter.js) into numbered pipes.
- Physics Refinements: Adjusted peg density, ball restitution, and gravity for satisfying gameplay.
- Pipe System: 5 distinct pipes with "Slot Machine" number spinning effects and persistent Bingo letter mapping.

2. Game Logic & Progression
- Bingo Rules: Balls landing in pipes mark numbers on the Bingo card. 5-in-a-row triggers a Win.
- Level System: Infinite progression (Levels 1-1000+).
- Dynamic Difficulty/Rewards: Coin rewards increase per level (100 + Level #).
- Win/Loss Conditions: "Level Pass" screen on Bingo; "Game Over" on running out of balls.
- Near Miss System: Logic to assist players when 1 away from Bingo.

3. Power-ups & Economy
- Coin System: Persistent currency across levels.
- Magic Number: Allows player to choose the next pipe number.
- Fireball: Special ball type with visual effects (active > 100 coins).
- Continue Option: Buy 10 extra balls for 1000 coins on defeat.

4. Visuals & UI/UX
- Branding: Title set to "Bingo Plinko Lucky Machine".
- Animations: High-quality SVG vector fire animations on pipes, sparkling particle effects.
- Modals: "LUCKY!" match notifications and "TRY AGAIN" prompts.
- Layout: Responsive design, fixed scrollbar issues on Level Pass screen, optimized footer and header alignment.
- Theming: Gold and Red color palette with "Premium" aesthetic.

5. Technical & Fixes
- Immediate ball removal logic upon pipe entry.
- Fixed modal display triggers and timing.
- Resolved "Magic Number" purchase bugs.
- White screen/Runtime error fixes in game loops.
